CareChain
Blockchain for Care

The digitalisation of the health ecosystem only just started but it is already on the wrong track!
When it comes to our lifes we can not copy the business models of data monetising social media companies but need to rethink ownership models as well as the foundational technical infrastructure.
We need to decentralise and distribute Self-Sovereign Health Records.

About

The CareChain consortium was founded by Provivus Health AB in 2017.
